Villagio Location

Lodha Group is presenting their new launch residential project “Lodha Bannerghatta Road” a dazzling project set to redefine luxurious living in Bangalore. Situated in the prime location of Akshayanagar, Begur on Bannerghatta Road this residential property offers a range of configurations from 2, 3 & 4 BHK luxurious apartments that serve different lifestyle preferences. The project is currently in its new launch phase and the best option is to seize it as your residential home in the best location in Bangalore. The property spans 10 acres of lush greenery promising a harmonious blend of urbanity and nature. With an impressive total of 550 units and possession scheduled for 2028 onwards. The Lodha Bannerghatta Road project presents an exclusive opportunity for selective homebuyers.

Lodha Bannerghatta Road Bangalore is built to provide residents with a lifestyle of unparalleled comfort and convenience. The project boasts several amenities including landscaped gardens, a fully-equipped fitness center, a refreshing swimming pool, and dedicated play areas for children. The infrastructure and construction work is focused on providing modern comforts and amenities to enhance the quality of life for its residents. Furthermore, the importance of security is paramount, with 24/7 surveillance and a gated community providing a sense of safety and peacefulness. In these thoughtfully planned residences inhabitants can enjoy the perfect combination of luxury, leisure, and securities making this project an ideal destination for those seeking an elevated living experience in Bangalore.

The project's prime location ensures effortless access to essential areas and amenities within the city. AMR Tech Park, Ozone Manay Tech Park, and Kalyani Magnum Tech Park nearby offer prime employment opportunities. Additionally, proximity to Yelachenahalli Metro Station (8 km) facilitates easy connectivity to retail and commercial hubs like Jayanagar and KR Market.

For IT professionals commuting to Electronic City, renting apartments in the vicinity is highly favored. Furthermore, renowned educational institutions such as VIBGYOR High, Samarthanam High School, and Jnana Vikas Public School, along with healthcare centers like Jayashree Hospitals and Vivekananda Hospital, are within reach.

Residents can indulge in recreational activities at Akshaya Nagar Park, Begur Lakeview Park, and Shantiniketan Tank Park nearby. Moreover, retail therapy waits at Royal Meenakshi Mall, Market Square Mall, and Vega City Mall, ensuring a well-rounded living experience.

From spacious living areas to well-equipped kitchens and stylish bathrooms, every aspect of the apartments is meticulously designed to offer convenience and luxury. Lodha Group's commitment to excellence ensures that residents enjoy a truly exceptional living experience, setting the project apart from others.
